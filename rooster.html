Rooster

The rooster is a barnyard animal that often appears on wind vanes and country-themed home decor. A rooster is an adult male chicken (Gallus gallus domesticus), which is the domesticated subspecies of the red jungle fowl. A rooster might also be called a cock (or if castrated, a capon), while a young male is a cockerel.
